FlashCash for shops

Receiving FlashCash payments is easy if you have a CheetaCard Payment Portal. The FlashCash online dashboard has all the facilities you need for registering and tracking payments.

Register now
How to register?

To begin accepting FlashCash, you need to first have a Cheetabank account and a CheetaCard Payment Portal. You can ask your nearest Cheetabank Branch Manager to create these for you. Once you get your assigned portal-code, you can create an account and register your Payment Portal on your FlashCash dashboard to begin receiving payments.

How do I receive payments?

To receive payments, click on Claim Notes in your FlashCash dashboard. You will get a form to enter all your Note IDs and their Unlock Codes. On entering the correct codes, the notes will be automatically added to you FlashCash Payment Portal account. Money from your portal can later be transferred into your main Cheetabank account for you to withdraw.
